About Hack Forge

Hack Forge is a platform built to showcase innovation and creativity. It challenges participants to think outside the box, solve real-world problems, and build cutting-edge solutions. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker, Hack Forge offers an inclusive environment to showcase your skills and grow.

Vision

To inspire and empower the next generation of innovators by providing a platform to learn, create, and compete.

Mission

To be an event that drives technological advancements and fosters collaboration between students.

Network

Innovation

frequently asked questions

  • Can I participate individually or is a team mandatory ?

    You can participate individually. But since our goal is to promote a sense of unity & teamwork, it is encouraged to enroll as at least a team of 2 or as a group of maximum 4 members.

  • What are the guidelines for team formation ?

    The members of a team can be from different branches, years or colleges. As long as they are a student of computer science (CSE, Ai&DS, IT, BCA etc.)

  • Will there be swag ?

    Along side certificates, mementos & of course the cash prize, participants & winners will receive swags.

  • Are Ai tools allowed ?

    Yes, the use of Ai tools is allowed. However, its use case must be explicitly citied whenever & wherever they are used.

  • Do I need to pay money to register for the Hackathon ?

    Yes, the ticket for an individual is Rs.250 & Rs.50 shall be added for every additional member.

  • How detailed must the idea/presentation be ?

    A clear & concise presentation is always preferred, as long as it portrays your idea properly.

  • What will the accommodation, facilities & hospitality include ?

    The students are to remain in the designated locations for the duration of the hackathon, internet access shall be available for everyone alongside access to power outlets, nonetheless it is advised for larger teams to carry extension boxes to accommodate multiple devices. The organizers shall provide lunch to all participating students, snack stalls shall also be present.
